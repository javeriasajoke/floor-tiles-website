Not only can we design and bring your creation to life, but our installations are second to none. Artis have over 20 years of experience within the flooring industry, with the past decade being dedicated to bespoke luxury vinyl tile installations.

All of our planks and tiles are finished with bevelled edges. Each edge is chamfered with precision, so when fitted, they make a ‘V’ in the join. This creates a more defined finish.
